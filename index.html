In addition, I became infatuated with different types of software including operating and applications software. But my curiosity did not end there, I wanted to explore all things related to information technology. I immersed myself into learning about networks, security, and information systems by enrolling in college courses during my senior year of high school. As I ventured towards secondary education, I reevaluated my interests and realized that I have a deep interest in the realm of software engineering and management.
